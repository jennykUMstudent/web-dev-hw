Costa Alegre Day Spa

Site Slogan

Welcome to Costa Alegre Day Spay
Our staff of 45 went through an intensive training program to bring you the best care possible.
We have mud and salt water pools with a capacity of 15, and 10 salt water float tanks.

Costa Alegre is a place to forget about the outside world. Because of this we ask phones to be silenced and customers to be curtious of one another.
We do have designated private and lounging spaces designated for phone usage.
Our day spa is ADA accessable, any accomadations needed will not be a problem.
If you have service animals, they are welcome! However, we ask they be calm and collected and use the designated grass areas on the facility.

Strawberry Puff Pastry Dessert

 Ingredients  Total Time  Oven Temp
 4  30 min.  400°F

Ingredients

  • 2 sheets of puff pastry
  • 1 cup crème fraîche
  • 2 tablespoons confectioners' sugar
  • 4 ounces strawberries

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F

  2. Cut 2 sheets of puff pastry in half. Bake 3 of the sheets in the oven for 18 min.

  3. Cut 4 oz. of strawberries into slices.

  4. In a medium bowl, whip 1 cup crème fraîche and 2 tbsp. confectioners' sugar until fluffy.

  5. Layer pastry, cream and 4 oz. strawberries; repeat on second layer. Add the last puff pastry on top.

  6. Dust with confectioners' sugar. Serve.

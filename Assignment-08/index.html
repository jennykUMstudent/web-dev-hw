ORIGAMI FLAMINGO

STEPS

  1. Choose a color of square origami paper.
  2. Fold the top right corner of the paper down to the bottom left corner.
    Crease & open again and repeat with top left corner to bottom right corner.
  3. Flip the paper over and fold the right side of the paper to the left side (hotdog style)
    Crease & open again and repeat with top side to bottom side (hamburger style).
  4. Using the creases you have made,
    bring the top three corners of the model down to the bottom corner.
  5. Fold top triangular flaps into the centre and unfold
  6. Fold top of model downwards, crease well and unfold
  7. Open the uppermost layer of the model, bringing it upwards and
    pressing the sides of the model inwards at the same time.
    Flatten down, creasing well.
  8. Turn model over and repeat Steps 5-7 on the other side.
  9. Fold outer edges into the center. Turn over and repeat on other side.
  10. Inside reverse fold the left side along the crease.
    Then fold down the head and beak.
  11. Open the right side and flatten out foot.
  12. Mountain-fold the back layer point backwards to the center.
    Flip over and repeat on other side.
  13. Final step: fold down top flaps into wings
  14. Optional: curl wings with pen/pencil

ABOUT THIS PROJECT

I chose to do an origami flamingo for a few reasons. The first being it's an easier one to start out on, and one where a person whose never done origami can succeed. It's currently June 21st 2019, which means the weather is warming up and summer is here. In summer, there tends to be a lot of flamingo items in stores, which is why a flamingo in particular came to mind.
I think this is an important project, since it's one almost everycan do and be proud of. Origami is something less common. My coworkers and I started doing origami when it got slow at work. To turn a piece of paper (and sometime even money) into a shape, creativity really shines through people.

LEARN MORE

It is not hard to learn origami, there are many videos, websites, apps, and even pictures, that show you step by step what to do.
